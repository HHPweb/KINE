Exercise is Medicine® On Campus at ECU

What is Exercise is Medicine® On Campus?

Exercise is Medicine® On Campus (EIM-OC) is designed to encourage students, faculty and staff to improve their physical fitness, health and wellness across campus.

Exercise is Medicine® On Campus Mission Statement:

To foster collaborative relationships and leadership on campus between exercise, health, and other disciplines that support the EIM-OC vision and goals.

Exercise is Medicine® On Campus Vision Statement:

To see all campus and community members across multiple disciplines discover, share, and adopt the principles of EIM-OC that will help change the culture of chronic disease prevention and management campus-wide.
